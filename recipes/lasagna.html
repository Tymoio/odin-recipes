Description

This homemade lasagna is layered with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  2. Boil noodles.
  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses.
  4. Bake at 375Â°F for 45 minutes.
